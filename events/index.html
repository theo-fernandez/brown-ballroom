Social Dances and Tournaments

Throughout the year, Brown hosts/attends many social dances and competitions. Social dances are a time to practice with and meet new people from nearby schools and community members from the providence area. Brown Ballroom hosts one social at the end of the fall semester called the Snow Ball, and one social at the end of the spring semester called the Phoenix Ball. Competitions are a chance to show off what you have been learning in class. BBDT attends ~3 tournaments a semester. Brown Ballroom also hosts an annual competition at the end of October.

Upcoming Events

Snow Ball

December 11 from 7-10pm | Alumnae Hall

Snow Ball is an annual social dance put on by the Brown Ballroom Dance Team. At the Snow Ball, there will be a group lesson in chacha and tango, followed by a performance by the members of the ballroom dance team. After that, there will be social dancing until the end. Admission is free for Brown/RISD students and $5 for everyone else. Everyone is welcome and encouraged to come, regardless of skill level or previous dance experience!

Phoenix Ball

Time TBD | Location TBD

Phoenix Ball is an annual social dance put on by the Brown Ballroom Dance Team. At the Phoenix Ball, there will be a group lesson in east coast swing and foxtrot, followed by a performance by the members of the ballroom dance team. Phoenix ball is also a graduation ceremony for graduating members of the ballroom community. After that, there will be social dancing until the end. Admission is free for Brown/RISD students and $5 for everyone else. Everyone is welcome and encouraged to come, regardless of skill level or previous dance experience!
