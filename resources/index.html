Weekly Practice Schedule

Brown Ballroom has weekly practices that everyone is welcome to attend. The weekly practice times and locations are in a google calendar, shown below for your convenience. Open practices are times to practice and meet the team, and classes are taught be professional coaches.

Here are links to the google calendars for newbie classes, bronzie classes, silver+ classes, open practices, and social dances. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us.

Socials and Resources

Follow our Instagram and Facebook pages to see announcements about upcoming events, as well as pictures of our dancers at recent events. We post videos from competitions to our Youtube page so you can watch current and past members dance and compete. Finally, you can go to our Google Drive to see past lessons in case you miss a day of classes.
